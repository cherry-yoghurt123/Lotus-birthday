Carly Rae Jepsen

On this fun and magical planet, i just knew that Carly Rae Jepson would be an icon. She is actually the queen of fun pop bangers and bops. You probably know Call Me Maybe and some of her most popular songs, but god is the rest of her discography underrated. The second song Fake Mona Lisa was my absolute jam in y9 (and still is). So please enjoy celebrating your birthday by shaking your ass to some carly.
